Magawa, sana kayanin ko, who i used to be. I can see it has taken your smile, as you stare out the window and watch it rain. I wonder how you must be feeling inside, i guess the rain reflects your outer side. I was standing by the edge of the water i noticed my reflection in the waves then i saw you looking back at me and i knew that for a moment you were calling out my name you took away my hero will you take away my pain.
